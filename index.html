About

Lucija is a Skopje-based photographer with three years of experience across events, documentary, and cultural work in North Macedonia.

Recent projects include the Skopje Street Food Festival, official sponsor photography for Vivas at a national Kids Olympics, and production photography for the independent theatre piece Kokoshka, with images shown at several festivals.

Her approach is documentary and people-first: authentic moments, real working settings, and a visual story told through a sequence of images rather than a single posed frame.
