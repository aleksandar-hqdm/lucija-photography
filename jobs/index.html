    Where to keep finding gigs

    Set these once and new opportunities come to her inbox. Then she is not starting from scratch each month.
    The set-once alert system (do these first)

    One-time registrations (be on the roster before a gig opens)

    • UNGM, one gateway for procurement across 32 UN agencies. Register free as an Individual Consultant, then save keyword alerts for "photography", "photographer", "videographer", "visual documentation". ungm.org
    • IOM supplier portal iom.int/business-opportunities and UNHCR supplier portal unhcr.org. Both have photographer rosters.
    • ImpactPool profile + job alerts for "photographer" and "visual communications", region Europe. impactpool.org
    • TED (EU tenders) account, save a search on CPV code 79961000 (photographic services) across GR, SI, HR, BA, RS, MK, AL, BG with email alerts. ted.europa.eu
    • Council of Europe EProc supplier account with keyword alerts "photography", "communications". eproc.coe.int
    • Photographers for a Cause, NGO-to-photographer matching, submit portfolio. photographersforacause.org

    Standing feeds and alerts

    • ReliefWeb jobs, the main humanitarian/NGO board. Search "photography communications visual", filter by each country, then turn the result into an RSS feed (no login) and read it in Feedly. reliefweb.int/jobs
    • Google Alert (set to daily): ("Western Balkans" OR Serbia OR "North Macedonia" OR Bosnia OR Croatia OR Albania OR Greece) AND (photographer OR photography OR "visual documentation") AND (tender OR consultant OR "open call") google.com/alerts
    • LinkedIn, save job searches for "photographer consultant" in Serbia, North Macedonia, Bosnia, Croatia, and follow UNDP, UNICEF, UNHCR, IOM, OSCE, Regional Cooperation Council, RYCO.
    • PhMuseum publishes a monthly round-up of open calls and paid photo commissions. phmuseum.com/grants
    • Cultural institutes: follow Goethe-Institut, Institut Francais, and British Council pages in each country. They post documentation calls that never hit the formal portals.
    Regional bodies and grants that fund whole-region work
    • Regional Cooperation Council (Sarajevo), summits and forums across the region. rcc.int/open_calls, press@rcc.int
    • RYCO (Regional Youth Cooperation Office), youth events across 6 countries. rycowb.org, office@rycowb.org
    • Western Balkans Fund, cross-border culture grants. Partner with an applicant NGO and be named as their project photographer. westernbalkansfund.org
    • European Fund for the Balkans and Balkan Trust for Democracy (German Marshall Fund), ask for grantee lists and pitch the funded organizations. balkanfund.org
    • Creative Europe / CC4WBs, EU culture funding open to Western Balkans residents, individual grants up to EUR 5,000. culture.ec.europa.eu
    • SALTO South East Europe, ask to be in the referral directory for youth-event documentation. salto-youth.net

    • North Macedonia: vrabotuvanje.com.mk, kariera.mk, civicamobilitas.mk/en/announcements, e-nabavki.gov.mk (public tenders)
    • Greece: kariera.gr, skywalker.gr, ngoheroes.gr, eprocurement.gov.gr (search "photography services")
    • Albania: duapune.com, albania.un.org/en/jobs, vacancies.osce.org, resourcecentre.al
    • Serbia: poslovi.infostud.com, civilnodrustvo.gov.rs (open calls), sajam.rs/en/calendar-2026 (Belgrade Fair, ~15 fairs/yr, one press contact), shootday.com/belgrade

    • Croatia: mojposao.hr, infozagreb.hr/en/events, croatian-photography.com, zaklada.civilnodrustvo.hr
    • Slovenia: mojedelo.com, cnvos.si/razpisi (NGO calls), fzs-zveza.si (photo competitions)
